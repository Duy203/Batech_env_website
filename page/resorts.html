Popular Resorts in Washington

Crystal Mountain

Located near Mount Rainier, Crystal Mountain is one of Washington's premier ski resorts. It offers a variety of terrain for all skill levels, from beginner slopes to challenging runs for advanced skiers and snowboarders. The resort also features stunning views of Mount Rainier and the surrounding Cascade Mountains.

Visit Crystal Mountain Website

Steven Pass

Stevens Pass is known for its diverse terrain and excellent snow conditions. It has a mix of groomed trails and backcountry options, making it a favorite among both skiers and snowboarders. The resort also hosts various events and competitions throughout the season.

Visit Stevens Pass Website

Mount Baker

Famous for receiving some of the highest snowfall in North America, Mount Baker is a haven for powder enthusiasts. The resort offers a range of terrain parks, bowls, and glades, catering to riders looking for an adventurous experience. Its laid-back atmosphere attracts a loyal following of snowboarders and skiers alike.
